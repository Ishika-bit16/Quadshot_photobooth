    About

    QuadShot is an interactive photobooth platform that allows users to capture and customize a four-photo layout. It features a collection of frames and stickers for creative editing. Users can conveniently download and store their photos for future use and sharing.

    How We Use Your Data

    • To allow you to capture and edit photos
    • To apply frames, filters, and stickers
    • To enable downloading and saving your photos

    We do not use your photos for any other purpose.

    Privacy Policy

    QuadShot does not collect personal information like name, email, or phone number unless you voluntarily provide it through the contact or feedback section.

    If you choose to contact us, we may collect your name, email address, and message only to respond to your queries or feedback.

    The photos you capture using the photobooth are temporarily processed to provide features like frames, stickers, and downloads. These photos are not permanently stored and are deleted after processing.
